  • How to get started with basic tools?

    You can get started in 5 mins. Simply follow the instructions.
    Step 1. Install Google Sheets add-on.
    Step 2. Connect your database.
    Step 3. Create and schedule a report.
    Step 4. Create a template.
    Step 5. Check out help center for help.

  • How do create a database connection?

    Go to Kloudio Connections and click on create a new connection. You will see a pop-up. Fill all the details such as connection name, database type, username, password along with Host address and Save it. Now you have successfully created a database connection

  • How do create a report?

    Please ensure that you have created a database connection prior to creating a report. nce you have created a database connection click on Kloudio Reports. Click on create report. Seleect the database connection followed by database/schema. Select particular columns as per needed and save it. Report will be created. Now you have successfully created a report

  • What are the different subscription plans ?

    There are four subscription plans namely
    1. Free trial Plan
    2. Starter Plan
    3. Pro Plan
    4. Premium Plan

  • What are the features of Trial plan?

    The user can opt for a 14 days free trial. For free trial we offer all the Pro features with 24/7 online support.

  • What are the features of Starter plan?

    The pricing for the starter plan is very simple and economic as after the free trial the pricing is $9 per user for both monthy as well as annual subscription. We offer features such as support for all databases, creation of connections for databases to form 10 reports cloud based along with online support. Click here to subscribe.

  • What are the features of Pro plan?

    Pro plan offers you features of Starter plan + much more. You will have access to refresh feature where you can set hourly, daily, weekly refreshes. You will also have access to features such as sharing and collaboration of 30 reports and uploading upto 30 templates. There is an additional feature of chat support where you can directly contact or tech support with online chat messages along with phone support. The most amazing thing about Pro plan is that all these features are available for just $29 per user for monthly subscription and even better for $26 per user per month for annual subscription. Click here to subscribe.

  • What are the features of Premium plan?

    Premium plan offers you features of Pro plan + much more. You will have access to 5 min and 15 min scheduled refresh feature in addition to hourly, daily, weekly refreshes. You will also have access to features such as sharing and collaboration of 100 reports and uploading upto 100 templates. Premium plan also provides you with dedicated account manager feature. Premium users will have access to 24/7 support. The most amazing thing about Premium plan is that all these features are available for just $149 per user for monthly subscription and even better for $99 per user per month for annual subscription. Click here to subscribe.
